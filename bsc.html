Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.)

Vinayakrao Patil College, Vaijapur

Course Overview

The Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) program focuses on developing scientific knowledge, analytical skills, and practical understanding. The course prepares students for careers in research, education, industry, and higher studies.

Subjects Offered

Physics

Study of mechanics, optics, electricity, magnetism, and modern physics with practical experiments.

Chemistry

Covers organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry, including laboratory-based learning.

Mathematics

Focus on calculus, algebra, statistics, and applied mathematics for scientific problem-solving.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed 12th (HSC) with Science stream
  • Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics required
  • Admission as per university and government rules

Laboratory Facilities

The college provides well-equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Computer Science to ensure hands-on learning and experimental knowledge.

Career Opportunities

  • Research Scientist
  • Teaching and Education
  • Laboratory Technician
  • IT and Data Analysis
  • Higher Studies (M.Sc., MCA, MBA)
