Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

Vinayakrao Patil College, Vaijapur

Course Overview

The Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) program is designed to provide students with strong knowledge in accounting, finance, business management, and economics. It prepares students for careers in commerce, industry, and professional courses.

Subjects Offered

Accounting

Financial Accounting, Cost Accounting, and Corporate Accounting fundamentals.

Business Management

Principles of management, organization behavior, and business strategy.

Economics

Indian economy, macro & micro economics, and economic policies.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed 12th (HSC) from Arts / Commerce / Science stream
  • Basic knowledge of Mathematics preferred
  • Admission as per university and government rules

Career Opportunities

  • Accountant
  • Banking & Insurance
  • Business Analyst
  • Company Secretary (CS)
  • Chartered Accountant (CA)
  • Higher Studies (M.Com, MBA)

Internship & Training

The college provides internship opportunities, industry visits, and practical exposure to enhance students’ professional skills.
